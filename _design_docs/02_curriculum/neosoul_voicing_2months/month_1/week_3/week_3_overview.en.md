# Week 3 — Chromatic Approach
> Curriculum: neosoul_voicing_2months · Month 1 · Parent: ../month_1_overview.md · dayKey: m1.w3

Neo-soul's signature isn't gripping a chord dead-on — it's **sliding in from a half-step above**. Like an elevator easing to a stop at a floor, you touch **Ab13** a half-step above the target, then push down a half-step to **G13** for a smooth landing. You only move the same shape one fret, so your hand stays comfortable.

## This week's goal
Get the **half-step slide approach** from Ab13 (the G13 shape +1 fret) to G13 into your hands, then slot that approach in front of the vamp's G13 to finish <mark>a chromatic-approach vamp</mark>. When the sliding landing feels natural, you've crossed the line.

## This week's journey
- **Day 1** — Ab13 grip. Move the G13 shape up one fret (`fretboard_diagram`).
- **Day 2** — Ab13→G13 slide. The feel of a half-step slip.
- **Day 3** — Slot the approach into the vamp. Dm9 → Ab13→G13.
- **Day 4** — Chromatic-approach vamp complete. With a sliding landing (Week 3 done!).

## Core feels
- **Approach from a half-step above.** Borrow the fret one above the target for a moment.
- **Slide the same shape.** The shape stays fixed; only one fret slips.
- **The landing is everything.** Touch it briefly, then settle straight onto the target.
