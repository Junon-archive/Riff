# Week 2 — Coloring with Tension
> Curriculum: neosoul_voicing_2months · Month 1 · Parent: ../month_1_overview.md · dayKey: m1.w2

The same Dm9→G13 changes color completely with a single **tension** — like spraying color onto a chord. **add9, 11, and 13** add another coat of paint, and **sus4** in particular (delaying the 3rd to the 4th) gives a slightly floating tension. When that 4th **settles down onto the 3rd**, you get neo-soul's signature melting resolution.

## This week's goal
Change the vamp's color with add9, 11, and 13, and get the move that **resolves the sus4 tension to the 3rd** into your hands to finish <mark>a tension-colored vamp</mark>. When you hear the sus4→3 melting resolution, you've crossed the line.

## This week's journey
- **Day 1** — The 9th color of add9/m9. Tell apart the ring when a 9th is added.
- **Day 2** — 11 and 13 tension. Get the 13th color into your hand.
- **Day 3** — sus4 tension → 3rd resolution. The melting move of G13sus→G13.
- **Day 4** — Tension vamp complete. With the sus resolution included (Week 2 done!).

## Core feels
- **Tension is color.** The 9, 11, 13 over the chord tones paint the mood.
- **Sus floats for a moment.** Enjoy the tension of a 4th delaying the 3rd.
- **Resolution is settling down.** It melts as the 4th drops a half-step to the 3rd.
