# Week 8 — Synthesis Graduation — Neo-Soul Comping
> Curriculum: neosoul_voicing_2months · Month 2 · Parent: ../month_2_overview.md · dayKey: m2.w8

At last, the two-month **graduation piece**. You melt everything you've learned — **tension voicings, chromatic approach, b3→3 smears, laid-back groove** — plus **top-note chord melody** into one Dm9→G13 vamp. Rather than learning something new, this week stitches the scattered pieces into a single progression.

## This week's goal
Melt tension, chromaticism, smear, laid-back, and chord melody into one progression to finish and record <mark>a full neo-soul comp</mark> that loops through without stopping. When the two bars connect naturally, you've reached the two-month finish line (neo-soul graduation).

## This week's journey
- **Day 1** — Element synthesis. Lay ornament over the comp in one bar.
- **Day 2** — Song structure. Build two bars (Dm9 comp + G13 + smear fill).
- **Day 3** — Rehearsal. From top to bottom without stopping.
- **Day 4** — Final graduation recording. The synthesis comp as one piece (neo-soul graduation!).

## Core feels
- **Connection over new tricks.** Stitch the learned pieces into one progression.
- **Don't stop.** Even if you slip, keep it rolling without breaking the flow.
- **In your own sound.** Record it and hear your own neo-soul.
