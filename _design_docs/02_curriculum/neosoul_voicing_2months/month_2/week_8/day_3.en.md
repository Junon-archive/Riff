# Day 3 — Rehearsal: all the way through without stopping

## ① Theory

Day three, and today is **rehearsal**. Loop the two bars from start to finish **without stopping**. <mark>Even if you slip, don't stop — move to the next beat; that's today's rule.</mark>

The heart of rehearsal is **flow**. Before accuracy comes the **unbroken repeat**. Even if your hand hesitates, the groove has to keep rolling. <mark>Not one perfect take, but ten non-stop takes complete the song.</mark>

The riskiest spot is the **Dm9 → G13 seam**. If your hand is late here, the flow breaks. Firm up this spot first and the run-through gets comfortable. <mark>When the seam is smooth, the two bars loop endlessly.</mark>

## ③ Today's practice (50-minute routine)

**0–10 min · Warm-up**
At **BPM 60**, sweep through the two bars once slowly. <mark>Wake yesterday's change and fill back into your hand.</mark>

**10–20 min · Brain training (firming the seam)**
Isolate just the **seam** and repeat Dm9 → G13 many times. Prepare your fingers ahead so they're not late.

**20–40 min · Real full run-through (BPM 72)**
Loop the two bars **without stopping**. Like the settle loop below, lay back on Dm9 at the end and rest one breath. <mark>Loop ten times without a stop and you've reached today's goal.</mark>
