# Week 6 — Grace Notes & Smears
> Curriculum: neosoul_voicing_2months · Month 2 · Parent: ../month_2_overview.md · dayKey: m2.w6

The same chord gains a whole expression from a single **grace note**. Tack a short note right before the target and let it slip in with a **pull-off**, and — like softly trailing off a sentence — the chord starts to 'talk.' This week you lay expression onto chords with grace notes and **pull-off descents**.

## This week's goal
Tack a short grace note before the target and let it slip softly with a pull-off to finish <mark>a pull-off descent ornament lick</mark> that adds expression to the chord. When the ornament doesn't stick out but blurs naturally, you've crossed the line.

## This week's journey
- **Day 1** — Grace note. From a short front note grazing into the target.
- **Day 2** — Pull-off descent. A descending ornament that flows as you lift the finger.
- **Day 3** — Lay ornament onto chords. Make a chord with expression.
- **Day 4** — Pull-off descent ornament lick complete. With a blurring expression (Week 6 done!).

## Core feels
- **Keep the grace note short.** Graze just before the target note.
- **Let the pull-off flow.** Don't snap — lift the finger gently.
- **Ornament is expression.** It adds a talking nuance to the same chord.
