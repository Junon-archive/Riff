# Day 4 — Complete! The pull-off descending grace lick (Week 6 complete)

## ① Theory

The final day of Week 6. You complete the **grace note** and the **pull-off descent** you built all week into a single **ornament lick**. <mark>It's a descending ornament lick that flows down over Dm with **pull-offs**.</mark>

The lick is four notes. From the 3rd string **fret 7 (R=D)**, flow down with a **pull-off** through **b7·5** and land on **b3 (F)**. <mark>You pick the first note once; your left hand streams the rest.</mark> This week's front notes and descents all live in this one line.

Today's goal is to complete this **ornament lick** smoothly at **BPM 72**. <mark>Not speed — when the pull-offs link without a break and the lick flows down like water, you've finished Week 6.</mark> First, learn where the lick travels.

## ③ Today's practice (50-minute routine)

**0–10 min · Warm-up**
At **BPM 60**, pick the lick's four notes (**R·b7·5·b3**) one at a time. Check the sound first, without pull-offs. <mark>Hear first whether the four notes link clearly in a descent.</mark>

**10–20 min · Brain training (pull-offs slowly)**
Now pick **only the first note** and stream the rest with **pull-offs**. If the sound cuts, put a touch more of that downward-scraping feel into your left finger.

**20–40 min · Real ornament lick (BPM 72)**
Repeat the two bars below. Bar 1 is the ornament lick; bar 2 lands on the **root (D)** and rests. <mark>Feel the flow of settling comfortably onto the root after the lick streams down.</mark>

## ④ Tips / Common mistakes

Here are just the mistakes that show up most as you complete the ornament lick.

▶ The 3rd string b7 and 5th are the descent path; the 4th string green b3 is the lick's landing.

- **The pull-off is weak.** Don't just lift the finger; scrape the string slightly downward to sound the next note.
- **The descent rushes.** Don't rush the four notes; stream them down evenly on the beat.
- **The b3 landing floats.** Fret the final b3 clearly and leave a resonance to settle down comfortably.
- **You raise speed first.** Drop to BPM 60 and complete the lick with the pull-offs linking without a break first.
