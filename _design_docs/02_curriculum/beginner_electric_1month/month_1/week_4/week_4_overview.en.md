# Week 4 — Finish a Song: Graduation, and the Next Door
> Curriculum: beginner_electric_1month · Month 1 · Parent: ../month_1_overview.md · dayKey: m1.w4

Graduation week at last. You'll expand the power-chord rhythm, link verse into chorus, and play <mark>a whole song end to end</mark> without stopping. A taste of pentatonics opens the door to your next journey, and on the final day you record the finished song to see four weeks of growth with your own hands.

## This week's goal
Give the power-chord rhythm some character, link the verse-to-chorus change without a gap, taste the pentatonic box, and on the final day record the finished song from start to end.

## This week's journey
- **Day 1** — Expand the power-chord rhythm (rests, syncopation, octaves).
- **Day 2** — Link the parts. Quiet verse → wide-open chorus.
- **Day 3** — A taste: minor pentatonic Box 1 + hammer-ons/pull-offs.
- **Day 4** — Graduate. Record the finished song + a 4-week BPM growth chart + where to go next.

## Core feel
- **Verse quiet, chorus wide open.** Dynamics make a song 'explode.'
- **Keep moving through mistakes.** Don't break the flow even if you slip.
- **Record the finished song and keep it.** Play today's you back to the you of four weeks ago. Next tracks: solo/scale, funk rhythm, chord building.
