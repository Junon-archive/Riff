# Week 1 — Wake Up Each Hand: From Posture to the Spider
> Curriculum: beginner_electric_1month · Month 1 · Parent: ../month_1_overview.md · dayKey: m1.w1

There's no song yet this week. Instead, you build the hands that make <mark>one clean sound</mark>. You start with posture and pick grip, then wake each hand separately — the right with downpicking and alternate picking, the left with the 1-2-3-4 chromatic. Sore fingertips are completely normal — calluses form in a few days.

## This week's goal
Strike the open 6th string evenly, fret the 1-2-3-4 chromatic with no buzz, get both hands ready with alternate picking, and on the final day play the spider chromatic across all six strings — clean, at 60–70 on the metronome.

## This week's journey
- **Day 1** — Meet the guitar. Posture, pick, tuning; even downpicking on one string.
- **Day 2** — Left hand's first steps. One finger per fret, the 1-2-3-4 chromatic.
- **Day 3** — Alternate picking. Even down-up motion.
- **Day 4** — Spider chromatic. Cross the strings, 6th → 1st.

## Core feel
- **Forget speed.** The only measure is clean tone with no buzz.
- **One finger per fret.** Fingertips up, pressing just behind the fret.
- **Sore hands are normal.** Calluses are the answer.
