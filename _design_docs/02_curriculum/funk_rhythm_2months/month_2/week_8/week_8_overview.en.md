# Week 8 — The funk machine recording challenge: fill the empty track with your groove
> Curriculum: funk_rhythm_2months · Month 2 · Parent: ../month_2_overview.md · dayKey: m2.w8

This week isn't for learning — it's a **stage.** Pull out the eight weeks' arsenal (motor, ghosts, accents, E9, three-string cutting, single notes, staccato, shuffle), assemble your own groove over an empty bass-and-drums track (E funk, BPM 78), and record it. Not a test but a celebration of filling that empty space with your rhythm.

## This week's goal
To complete and join a cutting part (verse) and a single-note riff (hook), play it whole over the empty backing track and record it, then diagnose it yourself against a strict checklist.

## This week's journey
- **Day 1** — Challenge orientation. Taking stock of the arsenal + choosing option cards.
- **Day 2** — Building the cutting groove. A verse part from three-string cutting + ghosts/accents.
- **Day 3** — Building the single-note riff. A hook part from single notes + staccato, joined to the cutting.
- **Day 4** — The final recording + self-analysis. Record it whole, diagnose, and recap two months.

## Core feels
- **This week is a stage.** A celebration of pouring out what you learned.
- **Join parts to make a tune.** Cutting (verse) + riff (hook).
- **The recording is the proof.** Keep two months' fruit and diagnose yourself.
