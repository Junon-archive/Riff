# Month 2 — High register, color, top notes: turning comping into art
> Curriculum: chord_building_2months · Month 2

In month 1 you learned to "assemble" chords (interval switches of the 3rd & 7th over a root). This month you paint **color and song** on top. Move the root up to the 4th string to open the **high register,** add color with 9th **tensions,** make the **top note flow like a melody** as chords change, and finally mix it all to **record your own comping.**

## This month's journey
- **Week 5** — 4th-string-root (D-shape) high-register triads. The start of the "top note" idea.
- **Week 6** — Adding color. Reaching the pinky to lay a **9th tension** onto m7·Maj7.
- **Week 7** — Real voice leading. Connecting a **top-note melody line** smoothly as chords change.
- **Week 8** — Chord-maker recording challenge. Mixing all the voicings you've learned to comp creatively, then record and analyze.

## What changes by the end
You go from someone who "grabs" chords to someone who **sings with the top note and colors with tensions.** You can design your own comping over a backing track.
