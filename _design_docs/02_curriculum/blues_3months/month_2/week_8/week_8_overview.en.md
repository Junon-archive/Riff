# Week 8 — Box 1 Lick Vocabulary → a First 12-Bar Solo
> Curriculum: blues_3months · Month 2 · Parent: ../month_2_overview.md · dayKey: m2.w8

Weave the scale, bending, and phrasing you've learned into a single **blues lick**. Lay these licks over the **12-bar** form and — for the first time ever — it becomes **your solo**. This is the week a hand that only knew the pentatonic starts to sing over 12 bars.

## This week's goal
Place a signature lick — opening with a whole-step bend and landing on the root on the way down — around the 12-bar form to play and record <mark>your first solo over 12 bars</mark>. When the licks flow naturally over the backing, that's your M2 graduation.

## This week's journey
- **Day 1** — Signature lick 1. A lick that includes the bend.
- **Day 2** — Signature lick 2. A lick that lands on the root on the way down.
- **Day 3** — Place the licks. Lay them over the 12 bars against the backing.
- **Day 4** — Play through and record the first solo (M2 graduation!).

## Core feels
- **A lick is a sentence.** Memorize it whole and place it, not note by note.
- **Open with a bend, close on the root.** One lick is a small question-and-answer.
- **Check it by recording.** Use your ears to hear if the first solo truly became a 'song'.
