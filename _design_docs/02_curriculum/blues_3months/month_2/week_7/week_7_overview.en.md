# Week 7 — Blues Phrasing: Call-and-Response, Space
> Curriculum: blues_3months · Month 2 · Parent: ../month_2_overview.md · dayKey: m2.w7

A good solo isn't notes — it's **speech**. You **ask** a short question phrase, rest a moment, then **answer** back. This week you learn to play in sentences with call-and-response, and to use the space that matters as much as the sound.

## This week's goal
Make <mark>a two-bar question and answer</mark> — bar 1 asks (left open, stopping on the 4th), bar 2 answers (landing on the root). When you can feel the space between the two phrases and the answer closes like a period, you've crossed the line.

## This week's journey
- **Day 1** — Short phrases. Make one bar of speech from 2–4 notes.
- **Day 2** — Call-and-response. A question phrase ↔ an answer phrase.
- **Day 3** — Leaving space. Make breath with a resting spot.
- **Day 4** — Phrasing, combined. Call-and-response complete (Week 7 done!).

## Core feels
- **A solo is speech.** Play in a sentence structure: ask, rest, answer.
- **Space makes the next line audible.** Rest, and the next phrase comes alive.
- **Questions open, answers close.** Stop on the 4th to open; land on the root to close.
