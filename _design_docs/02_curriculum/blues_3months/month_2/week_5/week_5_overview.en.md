# Week 5 — Minor Pentatonic Box 1 + the Blue Note
> Curriculum: blues_3months · Month 2 · Parent: ../month_2_overview.md · dayKey: m2.w5

The home of the lead is **Box 1** (5th fret). Add just one **blue note (b5=Eb)** and the minor pentatonic turns into the **blues scale**. That single note gives the blues its 'crying' color — one teardrop inside the scale.

## This week's goal
Run Box 1 up and down from the root A on the 6th string (5th fret), brushing past the blue note on the 5th string, 6th fret, to make <mark>a Box 1 where the blue note lives</mark> your own. When you can build a 3–4 note phrase inside Box 1, you've crossed the line.

## This week's journey
- **Day 1** — Carve Box 1's position. Etch it on the fretboard with a `scale_shape`.
- **Day 2** — Blue note (b5). Its added position and its 'crying' sound.
- **Day 3** — Short phrases. Build 3–4 notes inside Box 1.
- **Day 4** — Blues scale, combined. Box 1 + blue note complete (Week 5 done!).

## Core feels
- **Box 1 is the solo's playground.** Make this one spot completely your own.
- **The blue note is one teardrop.** Don't linger — brush past it and it comes alive.
- **No running up and down.** Start speaking in short phrases, not scales.
