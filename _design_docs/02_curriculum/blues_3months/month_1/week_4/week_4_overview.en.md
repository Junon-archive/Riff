# Week 4 — Turnaround → a Full 12-Bar Shuffle
> Curriculum: blues_3months · Month 1 · Parent: ../month_1_overview.md · dayKey: m1.w4

The **last two bars (the turnaround)** send the tune back to the top. Learn the **V-IV-I-V** knot and the 12 bars become a complete blues that loops without a break. Four weeks ago chords tripped you up — by the end of this week you'll play through **12 bars** and even record it.

## This week's goal
Tie off the 12 bars with a turnaround, add an intro/ending, and play through and record <mark>a complete blues that loops without a break</mark>. When one lap goes around without stopping, that's your M1 graduation.

## This week's journey
- **Day 1** — Turnaround. Send the tune back to the top with V-IV-I-V.
- **Day 2** — Intro/ending tags. Decorate the start and the finish.
- **Day 3** — Rehearsal. A full run-through of all 12 bars without stopping.
- **Day 4** — Play through and record the 12-bar shuffle (M1 graduation!).

## Core feels
- **The last 4 bars are the knot.** The turnaround carries the tune into the next lap.
- **Not stopping is finishing.** Miss a note but don't break the flow — keep it rolling.
- **Check it by recording.** Only your ears can tell you if the shuffle is alive.
