# Week 11 — Expanding Licks + Maturing Phrasing
> Curriculum: blues_3months · Month 3 · Parent: ../month_3_overview.md · dayKey: m3.w11

Weave **slides, bends, and vibrato** naturally into one lick, and give it room with **laid-back** timing that lies just behind the beat. Now your licks <mark>start to sound like speech</mark>, not drills. This is the week you tie the tools together into 'your own voice.'

## This week's goal
Complete an extended lick that enters with a slide, pushes up with a whole-step bend, and lands on the root with vibrato — <mark>a lick where the tools are woven into one sentence</mark>. When laid-back timing behind the beat comes out too, you've crossed the line.

## This week's journey
- **Day 1** — Enter with a slide. Glide in to open the lick.
- **Day 2** — Bend + vibrato combined. Make a crying lick.
- **Day 3** — Laid-back timing + dynamics. Room by lying behind the beat.
- **Day 4** — The combined expressive lick complete (Week 11 done!).

## Core feels
- **Weave the tools into one sentence.** Slide→bend→vibrato flow without a break.
- **Laid-back means lying behind the beat.** Play slightly late for ease and maturity.
- **Maturity comes from space and ease.** Don't fill everything — leave breath.
