# Week 9 — Chord Following: Landing on the Chord
> Curriculum: blues_3months · Month 3 · Parent: ../month_3_overview.md · dayKey: m3.w9

Instead of just running the pentatonic up and down, **land** on a chord's tone when it changes from **A7→D7** and your solo suddenly sounds like it's 'following the changes.' This week is the first step of **chord following** — responding to the chord from inside Box 1. (The whole-fretboard system is for the solo_scale journey next!)

## This week's goal
Land on the root A over the A7 bar and on the root D over the D7 bar to make <mark>the sound of following the changes</mark>. When the chord comes through even though you only changed the landing note inside the same box, you've crossed the line.

## This week's journey
- **Day 1** — Land over A7. Place phrase endings on the root/chord tones.
- **Day 2** — Land over D7. When the chord changes, move the landing note to D.
- **Day 3** — Land while switching. Change landing notes going A7↔D7.
- **Day 4** — Chord following, combined. The A7→D7 landing complete (Week 9 done!).

## Core feels
- **When the chord changes, home changes.** The landing note moves house with the chord.
- **Same box, just the landing note.** The chord comes through even without moving your hand.
- **The system is the next journey.** All five boxes and modes belong to solo_scale.
