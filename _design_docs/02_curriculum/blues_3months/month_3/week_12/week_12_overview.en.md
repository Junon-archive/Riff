# Week 12 — Rhythm-Lead Dialogue → Blues Graduation
> Curriculum: blues_3months · Month 3 · Parent: ../month_3_overview.md · dayKey: m3.w12

Finish the blues conversation on your own — play two bars of **comping** (the question), then answer with two bars of **solo** (the answer). This is <mark>a one-person blues band</mark>. Three months ago even the shuffle felt strange; by the end of this week you'll complete a whole tune alone and get sent off to the solo_scale journey next.

## This week's goal
Spread two bars of A7 boogie (comping = question) and two bars of Box 1 lick (solo = answer) across the whole 12-bar to play and record <mark>a 12-bar blues you complete on your own</mark>. When the comp↔solo switch is smooth, that's your blues graduation.

## This week's journey
- **Day 1** — Comp→solo switch. Switch your hands and your mind.
- **Day 2** — Two-bar dialogue. Trade comping ↔ lick.
- **Day 3** — Full 12-bar dialogue. A full run-through without stopping.
- **Day 4** — Final graduation recording + a solo_scale send-off (blues graduation!).

## Core feels
- **Ask with rhythm, answer with lead.** Comping is the question, soloing is the answer.
- **Become a band on your own.** Fill 12 bars by trading the two roles.
- **The next journey is the whole fretboard and modes.** To widen out, solo_scale is waiting.
